About Our Ministry

One Christ Ministry (OCM) is a religious organization based in Mamelodi East, Phumulong Extension 6, South Africa. Founded in 2019 by Reverend Bafana Vivian Radebe and Pastor Thembeni Margaret Radebe, our church was established to empower and build One Christian Community.


We serve the body of Christ through Evangelism, Discipleship, Teaching and Developing Leaders. Our foundation is built on the vision Rev. B.V. Radebe received in 2015 - to empower disadvantaged communities through the word of God.


One Christ Ministry is You, and You are One Christ Ministry! Together, we are the authors of this incredible story, for God's glory!
