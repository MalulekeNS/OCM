About Our Ministry

One Christ Ministry (OCM) is a religious organization based in Mamelodi East, Phomolong Extension 6, South Africa. Founded in 2019 by Reverend Bafana Vivian Radebe and Reverend Margaret Thembeni Radebe, our church was established to empower and build One Christian Community.


We serve the body of Christ through Evangelism, Discipleship, Teaching and Developing Leaders. Our foundation is built on the vision Rev. B.V. Radebe and Rev. M.T Radebe received in 2015 - to empower disadvantaged communities through the word of God.


One Christ Ministry is You, and You are One Christ Ministry! Together, we are the authors of this incredible story, for God's glory!

Leadership

Reverend Bafana Vivian Radebe & Reverend Margaret Thembeni Radebe are Founders and Lead Pastors of One Christ Ministry (OCM). The ministry has been existing in Phomolong Community for more than Six (6) years.


Rev. B.V. Radebe holds an MSc (Watercare) from the Tshwane University of Technology (TUT), and is experienced in Business Development, Project Management and Research & Development with a demonstrated history of working in the Research and Innovation Industry. He is skilled in technology development and implementation, and is currently pursuing PhD studies in Veterinary Science at the University of Pretoria.


Rev. M.T. Radebe holds a National Certificate in Auxiliary Nursing from Ekufundeni Nursing College and has more than 20 years’ working experience as a medical theatre specialist in both private and public hospitals. Both Rev. B.V. Radebe & Rev. M.T. Radebe hold a National Higher Certificate in Theology from Hebron Theological College.

Our Ministries

Preaching

Guiding the spiritual affairs of One Christ Ministry with wisdom and love.

Kids Fellowship

Developing and transforming children into God loving young people.

Youth Celebration

Leading the congregation into the presence of God through music and praise.

First Service

Equipping saints with the word of God for reproof, correction, and instruction in righteousness.

Prayer

Interceding on behalf of the church and individual prayer sessions with members.

Fellowship

Growing personal followers of Christ and spreading the teachings of Christ among nations.

Wellness Ministry

Compassion & Care

Giving love, opening our purse and sharing time with the destitute.

Providing mentorship about careers, professions and entrepreneurship.
